The silence cut open by fragmented transmissions.

The desolate rocks rumbling by in the void with the ruins of past civilizations. After waking up too late in the hunt for habitable planets, a lone astronaut is forced to consider their options: to continue with their mission in hopes that their home still lives or to succumb to the overwhelming solitude. Following a distant signal, explore a narrative rich environment from your spaceship to the many planets you encounter during your journey and solve puzzles to take you further into space in hopes that you will single handedly save your home and yourself. Will you keep your head when communication inevitably breaks down?
